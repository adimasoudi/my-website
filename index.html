About Me and My Background

My name is Adi Masoudi, and I am 26 years old. I began my undergraduate studies in Physics at Isfahan University of Technology in 2016. Studying physics provided me with a unique perspective on life and equipped me with invaluable theoretical and practical skills.

After completing my bachelor's, I pursued graduate studies in complex systems physics, also at Isfahan University of Technology. Unlike my undergraduate experience, my master’s work focused exclusively on theoretical projects. During this time, I developed expertise in a variety of advanced tools, including LSTM, GAN, GPR, multifractality, multiscale entropy analysis, functional data analysis, and ordinal networks.

My master’s thesis primarily explored financial market time series analysis, utilizing multiscale entropy analysis, multifractal analysis, and LSTM for time series prediction. I am highly interested in working in the field of data science and AI algorithms.
